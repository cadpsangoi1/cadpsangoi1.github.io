People who matter!

CA Dhirendra Sangoi

Founding Partner


Leading the team at DPS is Mr. Dhirendra Sangoi, the Managing Partner, who has been practicing Chartered Accountancy for more than 35 years and is at the helm the firm since its inception. He leads the Direct tax, Company Law, Audit & Assurance vertical and specializes in advisory as well as litigation practice.

Mr. Sangoi has advised various clients on business structuring such as mergers, de-merger, slump sale, conversion of Private Limited Companies to LLP, leading to optimal tax structures. His experience and knowledge on various commercial and real estate laws coupled with his business acumen gives him a cutting edge, which has been highly appreciated by the clients.

Mr. Sangoi has been the President of CVO CA & Cost Accountants Association for the year 1995-96. He has also been associated with various social initiatives such as Trusteeship in NGO’s - Shree Aath Koti Naani Paksh Sakal Sangh and Sadau Jain Sangh.





CA Shreyas Sangoi

Partner


He heads the Indirect tax space at DPS and is a qualified member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). His areas of functional expertise include GST, customs, excise, service tax, central sales tax, state VAT laws and foreign trade policy. He has the experience of working at a Tier-1 law firm for about 4 years.

He brings to the table, rich experience in handling matters related to Indirect tax, in both advisory and litigation domain. He has been involved in advising various MNC’s and Indian Conglomerates in sectors like manufacturing, financial services, entertainment, logistics, Information technology, gems and jewellery, real estate and infrastructure projects. Shreyas has conducted numerous Tax optimization exercises in varied sectors. He has also been involved in making representations to the Ministry of Finance and the State Governments in relation to various tax policy matters. Shreyas also looks after the team handling specialized internal audits.

Presently, Shreyas is the Indirect Tax Committee member of Western Indian Regional Council of ICAI, Chamber of Tax Consultants and Bombay Chartered Accountants Society. He is also a committee member of Youth Empowerment and Programmes Committee of CVO CA Association. Shreyas has been a speaker at various forums and has also authored numerous articles related to Indirect Tax in various journals and publications.
